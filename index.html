What is M&MG?

A pop-up carnival, built one city at a time.

Me & My Guys is a pop-up nightlife and culture brand that hosts "bring your crew" parties across Nigerian cities. The brand is the host — you and yours are the reason it works.

Every activation carries its own theme, its own vibe, its own city. This time, ladies and gentlemen, behold Calabar.
